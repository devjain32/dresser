For renter:
1. search for clothes
2. find something you love, reserve it, and wait for the owner to approve your rental
3. pay and get their number to get in contact with them
4. look great 
5. return to the owner by the designated time
6. repeat

For owner:
1. post the clothes and wait for a request 
2. look at the days they are requesting the item and approve or deny the request, then get in contact with the renter and drop the clothes
3. when time is up, get the clothes back and select on the app that the clothes have been returned
