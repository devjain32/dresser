A lot of the clothes we own are each worn a few times a year. They are also the most harmful to the environment. Jackets, blazers, and costumes contain more synthetic materials than our daily tshirts or jeans. It may be easier for us to start sharing these clothes to decrease the environmental consequences of fast fashion.

    Check out some of these facts from Business Insider:

  • Fashion production makes up 10% of humanity's carbon emissions, dries up water sources, and pollutes rivers and streams.
  • About 85% of all textiles go to the dump each year.
  • People bought 60% more garments in 2014 than they did in 2000.

It's our world. Let's work together to make it better.

How it Works

Shoppers

Rent your next outfit instead of buying.

Search and Reserve

Search the app by going through the categories. When you find something you love, add it to your cart and select the dates that you want it and your general location, so the owner can see where they need to drop it to. Wait for the owner to approve your request and then pay with your credit card through the app!

Get in Contact

Once your payment is processed in a few seconds, we'll give you the owner's number so you can get in contact with them. Coordinate a pickup with the owner, and make sure you're trying to stay safe by meeting them in a public setting! Now you have your product for the designated days that you reserved it.

Drop it Back

At the end of your rental period, coordinate with the owner about when to pick the item back up. Don't worry about washing it, as that is included in your purchase! Again, make sure that you stay safe by dropping the product in a public location. When you've dropped it off, go to the app and confirm that you have dropped off the product.




Owners

Do you have some clothes you want to put onto the platform? Here are the steps.

Post and Wait

Post your favorite clothes on the platform and carefully choose the price you would like to rent it at. It will also ask you what you value the product at as well as the cleaning fee you would like to charge. Now just wait for a request!

Approve and Drop

When someone reserves your item, check the days they've requested it and the location they want the product. Approve it if that works for you! The customer will then get your number and contact you! Coordinate the pickup.

Get them back

Once the rental period is over, go into your app and select that you have received the clothes and you are satisfied with their condition. If you aren't, call us! After that, wash them and throw them back up on the platform!
