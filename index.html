why dresser?

you could sell clothes online. so why pick us?

posting

photographing and uploading clothes with relevant tags and accurate descriptions takes time, but dresser will handle that work and input all correct item tages to our app for customers to browse and buy

expand your customer base

an independent website not only takes time and money to maintain, but it may not reach potential customers without expensive advertising

dresser advertises on your behalf, from physical ads to social media

quick delivery

unlike national-chain thrifting sites, we provide one to two day delivery, so you can sell to online custoemrs looking for last-minute items, peaking shoppers' interest in your stores

how we get you started

upload

a dresser team member will upload your inventory pieces we think will sell well through the app, taking pictures and loading item information

pick-up and drop-off

when a customer buys an item and selects a date, we will pick the item up from you, sanitize, quality check, and deliver it to the customer

returns

if the customer isn't satisfied with the item, we offer free returns and will drop the item back to you and repost it to the webiste
